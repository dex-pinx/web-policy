Privacy Notice

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to check our practices regarding how we handle your personal data. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please stop using or registering the service or visiting our website. By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you use our Services, including but not limited to:

  • Account Registration: Email address
  • Renting Items: Name, email address, postal address, delivery address, lease duration, and financial information (such as credit/debit card details and billing history).
  • Customer Support: Email address or phone number when you contact us with inquiries or requests.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect certain information automatically when you use our services, including:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Interaction data, analytics, and log files to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information only as permitted by applicable laws, including:

  • To provide, personalise, and improve our Services.
  • To process transactions, including payment processing and order fulfillment.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, orders, and inquiries.
  • To ensure security, detect and prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Personal Information

We may share your personal information with external third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Payment Processing: To facilitate secure financial transactions.
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors that assist in operating our Services (e.g., customer support, payment gateway provider, delivery, IT services).
    • Paypal
    • Google Analytics
  • Legal Compliance: When required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

4. User Rights Regarding Personal Information

  • Access to Information: You have the right to request access to your personal information held by Letn, except in cases where legal exceptions apply.
  • Correction of Information: You may make corrections or updates to your personal information records if you find any inaccuracies.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent for the use or disclosure of your personal information.

5. Personal Information Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Data that is no longer needed is anonymised or deleted.

  • When you request to delete your account, your personal information will be immediately anonymised and archived for 7 days. During this period, you can restore your account with all data intact by contacting us. After 7 days, all personal information will be permanently deleted from our systems and cannot be recovered.
  • To delete your account, open the mobile app and navigate to [Personal Tab] → [Settings] → [Account Management] → [Delete Account].

6. Notice at the Time of Data Collection

  • When collecting personal data (e.g., during account registration or rental transactions), a clear notice about what data is collected and for what purposes will be provided.
  • If using third-party services outside Canada (e.g., for payment processing), you will be informed and may request details on how these providers collect, use, disclose, store or handle data.

7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse.

8. Scope of Privacy Protection

Our privacy applies only to our services. If you follow links to any external websites or services, their privacy policies will govern their data handling practices. Letn is not responsible for how third parties collect or use your information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
